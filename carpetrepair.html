Hallway Damage

A pet destroying an area of carpet is a repair we handle frequently. We start by sectioning the damaged carpet with clean-cut, lines and then install a donor piece, a process known as a bonded insert. We often use carpet from a closet, or 'attic stock' if available, for this repair. Additionally, we replace all carpet from a closet or other area with a simular carpeting.
