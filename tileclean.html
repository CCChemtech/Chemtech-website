How does Chem Tech handle dirty tile?

Chem Tech addresses dirty tile with a meticulous and efficient approach. They begin by assessing the tile and grout's condition to tailor their cleaning strategy. Next, they apply specialized pre-treatment solutions to loosen accumulated dirt and stains. Using advanced equipment, they perform a deep clean with professional-grade scrubbers and high-pressure rinsing, ensuring that both tiles and grout are thoroughly cleaned. For grout, they apply dedicated cleaning solutions and sealers if needed to protect against future staining. Finally, Chem Tech conducts a thorough inspection to confirm the cleanliness and ensure the area is properly dried. This comprehensive process, combined with their expertise and advanced equipment, ensures that your tiles are restored to their optimal condition.
